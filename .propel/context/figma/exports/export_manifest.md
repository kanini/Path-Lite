- [ ] All 45 screens/states are present in Figma

- [ ] Touch targets ≥44×44pt on all interactive elements
- [ ] AIVoiceIcon three-state design visible in AI Active/Listening states
- [ ] StatusBadge five variants visible in Patient Dashboard states
